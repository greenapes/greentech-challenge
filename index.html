Welcome to the jungle!

You are about join the Lund University Sustainability Challenge, to see how sustainable you are and how sustainable you can be. So team up, share your green actions, earn points for your positive impact and enjoy.

What's in for you? Well, you get to understand the ease of a sustainable lifestyle, enjoy a challenge with your peers and, if your team should win, you will be awarded a free fika at Ariman's Cafe.

Just follow the steps below to sign up for the two week sustainability challenge!

  1. Have an iPhone? Download greenApes from the App store. Otherwise sign-up on greenApes.com
  2. Contribute to the success of your faculty team, share your sustainable actions on greenApes including a photo of the action and your unique team's #hashtag at the end of your comment*.
  3. Check all the actions of your team, friends and participants! Follow the “LU Challenge” profile in the “Communities” section of greenApes.
  4. Enjoy, mingle with other sustainable students from Lund University and get your free fika at Ariman’s if you manage to win the challenge!

*IMPORTANT! Join the Facebook group Lund University Sustainability Challenge and specify the unique #hashtag of your team. On the page you can also post questions, get more detailed information and be updated on the current score!!
